Because my alma mater and my employer have been so widely mythologized, I know that there are those who are curious about what it's like to be in my position.…

I'll be sharing bits & pieces of my experience from time to time, with the goal of showing that I'm an imperfect human with plenty of struggles as well as successes, and as a way of partially paying forward my good fortune to the world at large.
